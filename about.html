About Us

We are a group of high school students interested in CS topics. For PennApps XVIII, we decided to focus on heart health and different ways to prevent heart attacks. In addition to constructing this educational website, we also developed an app for users to record their exercise and a game to demonstrate target heart rates.

Ultimately, we aim to integrate all three elements into a fully functional prevention and detection system to improve the safety of all users, especially older Americans.
