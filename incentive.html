Rewards

We get it, staying fit isn't always easy...

We'd like to encourage you to keep up your exercise habits (or start some new ones), so your great efforts can lead to great rewards!

Rules

  1. Each day you exercise, enter your hours into our accompanying app! (Link coming soon)
  2. Weekly raffles will be held every Sunday.
  3. 5 winners will be chosen at random of those who enter hours.
  4. Prizes will include gift cards to restaurants like Chipotle and Shake Shack, Premium opportunities for goods like 3-month Amazon Kindle Unlimited, and live concert tickets!
