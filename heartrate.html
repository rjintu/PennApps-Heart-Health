Your Target Heart Rate

Your heart beats at a relatively constant rate, but changes depending on a variety of factors. Things that affect your heart rate include age, gender, exercise, and genetics. The normal resting heart rate is 60-100 beats per minute, but children tend to be on the higher side.

Each person also has a maximum heart rate, which is the highest pulse that person can get. To estimate this, use this formula:

Estimated Maximum Heart Rate = 220 - Your Age
